# Epic: AI Resume Adaptation Engine

## What
Adapts parsed resume content to align closely with job descriptions through context-aware rewriting.

## Why
Matches candidate qualifications with JD requirements to maximize ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems) and recruiter visibility.

## How
- Send parsed resume sections and target job description to the Gemini API.
- Use tailored prompts instructing the AI model to rephrase experience metrics using target keywords while strictly preserving the integrity of achievements.
